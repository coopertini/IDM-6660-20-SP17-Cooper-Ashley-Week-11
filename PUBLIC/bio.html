About Me

Hello!

I'm Ashley Cooper, Cooper for short. I've been working as a graphic designer for around seven years and loving every second of it. If someone told me way back when that I'd end up as a designer I would have been floored. Starting out in IT, I've always prefered the technical side of things rather than the design objectives. But as I started out in my first job, my boss recognized very quickly that I had a knack for design and helped push me in that direction. I'll never look back.
